ABOUT US


From product supervisors to plant and project engineers, foodprocessing-technology.com is the site trusted by the international food processing industry for up-to-date information on new products, services, trends and more. Foodprocessing-technology.com has one of the most comprehensive and detailed listings of food processing equipment suppliers, products and services, free white paper downloads and company press releases, making it the key point of reference for the industry. With a global reach, targeting key areas such as Europe, the US and Asia, this long-standing title has been able to penetrate a market no other such title has. Working alongside our sister publications packaging-gateway.com,we are able to reach further into this market, bringing you the processes further down the supply chain that affect how food manufacturing takes place.
